Explore the Factors


Each year the United Nations publishes the World Happiness Report ranking over 150 countries based on citizen perceptions of happiness across various lifestyle measures. These measures are analyzed and grouped into 6 factors adding to each countries overall score.


The Factors: GDP per Capita • Social (and Family) Support  • Life Expectancy • Freedom • Generosity • Government Corruption
