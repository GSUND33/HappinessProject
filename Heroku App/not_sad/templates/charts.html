6 Factors Most Correlated with World Happiness

Happiness and GDP

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is the annual value of all goods and services a country produces. This number is normally calculated independently by country and by a national government statistical agency.

Happiness and Social Support

Social Support is defined by the same survey results of the Gallup World Poll data. The average of all answers determines the outcome of this factor per country.

Happiness and life Expectancy

The estimate for Life Expectancy represents the average number of "healthy" years a child at birth is estimated to live. It's calculated by the World Health Organization (WHO) based on over 100 different health factors.

Happiness and Freedom

Freedom is based on the average response to the Gallup World Poll question: "Are you satisfied or dissatisfied with your freedom to choose what you do with your life?"

Happiness and Generosity

Generosity is based on the response to the Gallup World Poll question: "Have you donated money to a charity in the past month?"

Happiness and Government Corruption

Government Corruption is based on the average response to the following 2 questions: 1) "Is corruption widespread throughout the government?" and 2) "Is corruption widespread within businesses?"
